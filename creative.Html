Turtle Fun Facts

Turtles are fascinating creatures! Here are some fun facts about them:

  • Turtles are one of the oldest reptile groups, dating back to the time of dinosaurs over 200 million years ago.
  • Most turtles can retract their heads and limbs into their shells for protection.
  • The temperature of the nest where turtle eggs are incubated determines the gender of the hatchlings. Warmer temperatures typically produce females, while cooler temperatures produce males.
  • Some species of turtles can live for over 100 years!
  • Turtles have excellent underwater vision but see poorly on land.
  • Many species of turtles are endangered due to habitat loss, pollution, and illegal trade.
  • Sea turtles are known for their incredible migrations, traveling thousands of miles between feeding and nesting grounds.

Next time you see a turtle, remember these amazing facts about them! Consider donating to the turtles.
